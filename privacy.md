# Privacy Policy for Commercials

**Last updated:** May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Commercials** mobile application ("the App," "we," "us") handles your information.

## Who we are

Commercials is developed by **Ian Sidman** ("the developer"). You can reach us at **iansidman@gmail.com**.

## TL;DR

- We do not run our own servers. We do not collect your name, email, or contacts.
- The apps and categories you choose to block are managed by Apple's Screen Time framework on your device. We do not see them, store them, or transmit them anywhere.
- We show ads from Google AdMob. AdMob may collect a device-level advertising identifier and basic usage data to serve and measure ads. You control this with the standard iOS tracking prompt.

## What information we collect

**Information you provide to us:** None. Commercials does not have user accounts, sign-up, or any forms.

**Information collected automatically by third parties:**

- **Google AdMob** is integrated into the App to show rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may receive:
  - Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), only if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt
  - Approximate region (derived from IP)
  - Device type, operating system version
  - Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks, video completion)

  AdMob's data practices are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and AdMob's terms.

**Information that stays on your device:**

- The list of apps and categories you choose to block, your unlock-duration setting, and your ad-count setting are stored locally using Apple's `FamilyControls`/`ManagedSettings` frameworks. These never leave your device and we have no access to them.

## How information is used

The third-party data described above is used by Google AdMob to:
- Serve advertisements (including personalized ones, if you allowed tracking)
- Measure ad performance
- Pay the developer for served ads

We do not combine or analyze AdMob data ourselves.

## Sharing

We do not sell your information. The only third party receiving data is **Google AdMob**, as described above.

## Your choices and rights

- **Tracking:** You can deny tracking at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Commercials. Ads will still serve, but they will be non-personalized and lower paying.
- **Deletion:** Uninstalling Commercials from your device removes all locally stored data, including your blocked-apps list.
- **AdMob:** You can manage Google ad preferences at [adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com).
- **California, EEA, UK residents:** You have additional rights under your local laws (access, deletion, correction, portability, opt-out of "sale" or "sharing" — note we don't sell or share data outside what's described above). Contact iansidman@gmail.com to exercise any right.

## Children

Commercials is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has used the App, contact us and we will help.

## Security

We do not host any user data on our own servers, which limits the surface area for breaches. AdMob applies its own industry-standard security practices.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance.
