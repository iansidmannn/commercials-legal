I picked 1 ad but I'm watching 2?

Yeah, Google does that sometimes — they pack two short videos into a single ad slot (they call it an "ad pod"). We can't really stop them.

How do I change the number of ads?

It's at the top of the home screen — but to change it, you'll need to unlock your apps first. That's on purpose, to prevent people from getting their apps back early.

How do I change which apps are blocked?

They're listed at the bottom of the home screen — but to adjust them, you'll need to watch your ads first. Same reason as above, to prevent people from unblocking apps without doing the work.

Is Commercial watching what I do?

No! Everything is stored on your iPhone. Nothing about your activity ever leaves your phone. Privacy Policy has the full breakdown.

How do I delete my data?

There's no account to delete — everything lives on your phone. If you uninstall the app, it all goes with it.
